Faqs

How is Data Talk different from other analysis tools? ▼

Unlike BI tools that require SQL knowledge or complex spreadsheet formulas, Data Talk allows you to analyse data using plain English. Our AI model understands context and automatically chooses the best visualisations for your insights.

Is my data secure and private? ▼

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We don't use your private data to train our global models, and you have complete control over your datasets at all times.

Which file formats are supported? ▼

Currently, we support CSV and Excel (.xlsx) files. We are actively working on adding support for more formats and direct database connectors in the near future.

Can I export my charts and reports? ▼

Yes! You can download any generated chart as a high-quality PNG image. You can also pin them to your personal dashboard for later reference or presentation.
